NVIDIA-IT co-engineering to scope-lock an OpenShell MVP (Jul 13-17,
2026):

- **Two paths**: Workstation and infrastructure.
- **Collaborators**: Product, Consulting, Eco-system Engineering, and
  AI Engineering from the Red Hat side.
- **Goal**: scope-lock the MVP — define what ships and what doesn't,
  with a support-scope contract as one of the open action items.

E2E testing matrix doc created during this cycle to support the
co-engineering work.
