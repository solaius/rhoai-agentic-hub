Correction guard for field conversations and positioning docs: the
2026-04-17 OCC/Fed/FDIC revised MRM guidance **rescinds** the SR 11-7
lineage and **explicitly excludes** generative/agentic AI from its scope,
with an RFI pending. Bank expectations for agent inventories are real
but MRM-habit-shaped, not regulator-mandated — position the registry as
meeting inventory *practice*, not SR 11-7 *requirement*. Evidence and
sources: [research/10-requirements](/features/agent-registry/research/10-requirements.md).
