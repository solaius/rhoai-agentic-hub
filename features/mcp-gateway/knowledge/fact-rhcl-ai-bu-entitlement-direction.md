Owner statement (2026-07-10, during the first RHCL hub refresh gate): RHCL
has moved from the OpenShift business unit to the AI business unit. Though
not fully confirmed, the expected direction is that MCP Gateway shares all
of the same entitlements as MaaS.

As forward guidance this supersedes the RHOAI-RHCL support agreement's
current scope (unlimited-token and token-metrics entitlements applying to
MaaS only, with MCP and Agents excluded); rate limiting for AI workloads
and Authorino remain covered either way. The RHCL hub's entitlement section
(understand/rhcl-context.html) now states the BU move and the pending
parity direction; confirmation is tracked as an open question on
plan/open-questions.html. Update this fact and the hub page when parity is
confirmed or refuted.
