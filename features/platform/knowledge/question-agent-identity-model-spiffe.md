The messaging (see [fact-agentic-ai-messaging-position.md](/features/platform/knowledge/fact-agentic-ai-messaging-position.md)) and the AI Gateway F2F's tiered identity decision (Tier 2: SPIRE/Authbridge — restricted: [decision-ai-gateway-f2f-architecture.md](/restricted/features/platform/knowledge/decision-ai-gateway-f2f-architecture.md)) both point at SPIFFE/SPIRE, but the monolith still lists the choice as open as of 2026-07-05. Treat as tentatively SPIFFE/SPIRE pending confirmation that no alternative is still under consideration.
